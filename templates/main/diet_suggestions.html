🌍 General WHO Dietary Guidelines

🥬

Fruits & Vegetables

At least 400g (5 portions) of fruits and vegetables per day. Potatoes and starchy roots don't count.

🫘

Whole Grains & Legumes

Include whole grains (wheat, rice, oats), legumes (lentils, beans), and nuts in your daily diet.

🧂

Limit Salt

Less than 5g (about 1 teaspoon) of salt per day. Use iodized salt when possible.

🍬

Limit Sugar

Free sugars should be less than 10% of total energy intake. Reducing to below 5% provides additional benefits.

🫒

Healthy Fats

Total fat should be less than 30% of energy. Prefer unsaturated fats (fish, nuts, olive oil) over saturated fats.

💧

Stay Hydrated

Drink at least 2-3 liters of water daily. Increase intake in hot weather or during physical activity.
